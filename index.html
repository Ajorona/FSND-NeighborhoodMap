Nine and One

  • A quick way to learn about a small part of Northern California

Welcome to Nine and One. I'm an educational app that will tell you a little bit about the bay area, its regions, and counties + Santa Cruz (not technically the bay area). There are two panels for you to mess with. Select from the dropdown list in the left panel to highlight markers by region. (North Bay, East Bay, South Bay, Peninsula, and San Francisco proper) to get a feel for the spread of the bay area. After all, it is at least a 2 hour drive from Bodega Bay to Santa Cruz. Or, you can select from the right panel to see the location of landmarks in specific counties with weather info and a nice little view. Click a marker on the actul map to see it bounce and give a little information about a landmark you might want to visit in the Bay Area, info courtesy of the Wikipedia API.
